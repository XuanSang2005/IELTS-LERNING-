# Plan — The Atlas of Mistakes (Phase 1 skeleton)

Build the `/atlas` page skeleton so the new nav item resolves to a real route,
with 10 seed specimens distributed across the five "family" taxonomies. Phase 1
is the ship-it-tomorrow slice: masthead, family index, searchable specimen list,
and colophon. No interactive Map view, no per-entry permalinks, no submission
flow — those are Phase 3.

## Concept (from conversation)

The Atlas catalogues common Vietnamese-English IELTS errors as numbered
"specimens" in a naturalist-atlas format. Five families of error origin:

1. **Translations Literal** — word-for-word from Vietnamese
2. **False Cognates** — familiar-looking words with different meanings
3. **Phantom Collocations** — natural-sounding but nonexistent pairings
4. **Register Drift** — casual words leaking into formal writing
5. **Structural Echoes** — Vietnamese syntax in English dress

Each specimen: PL. number, family tag, frequency dots, band cost, wrong/right
pair, founder's marginal note, see-also cross-references.

## Design contract

**Masthead** (same pattern as tests/dashboard/lexicon/grammar):
- Mono label: `◆ ATLAS OF MISTAKES · VOLUME I`
- Fraunces headline: *"The atlas of mistakes."*
- Italic blockquote: *"Every error is a map of the language you haven't yet learned."*
- Polaroid right (reuse `/images/editions/edition-02.jpg` with `tint="claret"`)
- Full-width `border-b-2 border-line` separator, `pb-16 pt-11` inner padding

**Family index** (5 cards, grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-5):
- Mono label: `VOL. I — V`
- Fraunces name, italic tagline, mono count ("02 specimens" in Phase 1)
- Each card rotates `rotate-[-1.2deg]` / `rotate-[1.2deg]` alternating
- Click → filters the specimen list below

**Filter bar**:
- Mono label `SEARCH · 10 SPECIMENS`
- Family chips (ALL · I · II · III · IV · V), ink-fill active state
- Search input, Fraunces italic placeholder ("wrong phrase, right phrase…")
- Right-aligned count

**Specimen card** (per entry):
- Two-column grid md:grid-cols-[110px_1fr]
- Left column: `PL. NN` (mono claret), frequency dots (●●●●○), band cost (−0.5)
- Right column:
  - Family tag (mono uppercase)
  - Wrong line: `✗` + strikethrough Fraunces italic, claret
  - Right line: `✓` + Fraunces bold, ink
  - Horizontal hairline
  - Founder's note: border-left-2 claret, Fraunces italic 17px
  - Cross-references: mono small `SEE ALSO · № 012`
- Border-b hairline between entries

**Colophon** (footer of page):
- Mono label `§ II · COLOPHON`
- Italic paragraph explaining the volume (founder-authored voice)
- Link to `/pricing` ("Enter the programme to submit a specimen")

## Content — 10 seed specimens

Must cover all 5 families, 2 per family, drawn from common Vietnamese IELTS
errors. Band cost range 0.25–1.0. Frequency range 2/5 – 5/5.

Family I (Translations Literal): make homework; since + bare noun
Family II (False Cognates): sympathy (empathy/pity); actual (real/current)
Family III (Phantom Collocations): crowded traffic; heavy study
Family IV (Register Drift): kids in Task 2; stuff in essay
Family V (Structural Echoes): "Because...so..."; "Although...but..."
